Virgin Voyages has quickly built a reputation for doing cruising differently. From its adults-only policy to its modern design and dining approach, it’s not trying to be everything to everyone—and that’s exactly the point.

But is it right for *you*?

## What Makes Virgin Voyages Different
Virgin Voyages focuses on:
- Adults-only sailing (18+)
- Included dining at multiple restaurants
- No traditional cruise buffets
- Modern entertainment and design

The experience feels more like a boutique hotel at sea than a traditional cruise.

## Who Tends to Love Virgin Voyages
- Couples without children
- Solo travelers who enjoy social environments
- Travelers new to cruising who dislike rigid schedules
- Guests who value flexibility and style

## Who It May Not Be Ideal For
- Families traveling with children
- Travelers who prefer formal nights
- Guests seeking traditional cruise programming

## Cabin and Onboard Experience
Cabins are designed with comfort and function in mind, while onboard spaces encourage exploration without feeling overwhelming.

Dining is a standout feature, especially for travelers who don’t want assigned dining times.

## Expert Insight
Virgin Voyages works best when expectations match the experience. Travelers who choose it intentionally tend to love it; those who book it assuming “all cruises are the same” often don’t.

## Final Thoughts
Virgin Voyages isn’t about cruising more — it’s about cruising differently. For the right traveler, it can be an exceptional fit.
