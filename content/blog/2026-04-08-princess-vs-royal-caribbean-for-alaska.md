Princess and Royal Caribbean are two of the most popular cruise lines in Alaska, but they cater to different travel styles. Choosing between them isn’t about which is better — it’s about which fits *you*.

## Princess Cruises in Alaska
Princess is deeply rooted in Alaska travel.
- Glacier-focused itineraries
- Strong enrichment programming
- CruiseTour options into the interior

It’s ideal for travelers who want destination-first experiences.

## Royal Caribbean in Alaska
Royal Caribbean brings a more modern onboard experience.
- Larger ships
- More onboard activities
- Great for families and multigenerational groups

The ship itself plays a bigger role in the vacation.

## Key Differences to Consider
**Pace**
- Princess: slower, more immersive
- Royal Caribbean: energetic, flexible

**Onboard Focus**
- Princess: learning and scenery
- Royal Caribbean: entertainment and amenities

## Who Each Line Is Best For
- **Princess:** couples, empty nesters, explorers  
- **Royal Caribbean:** families, active travelers  

## Expert Insight
In Alaska, the right cruise line enhances the destination instead of distracting from it.

## Final Thoughts
Both lines offer incredible Alaska experiences — the best choice depends on how you want to experience the journey.
